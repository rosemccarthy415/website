kwistuup.net - Mostly resources with all NCN dialects created by Adam Werle, updated reasonably frequently.
toquahtlanguage.com - The Toquat people also speak the West Barkley dialect of NCN. Lots of information and lessons to be found on this site.
hinatinyis.com - Hinatinyas is an East Barkley dialect learner and teacher. Her website is current and has lots of unique resources that can be helpful for West Barkely learners too. Hinatinyas also does a weekly drop in NCN language twitch stream that you can find here.
Hišukʔaaqƛin huuḥtakšiiḥ - A facebook group run by Huḥtik for her West Barkley language classes. Lots a good recordings for survival phrases!
I want to learn the Nuu-chah-nulth Language - Another facebook group. Often used to ask people in the group for a word or phrase translation.
Tseshaht Language - A facebook page for Tseshaht language learners. Language lessons taught by Čiisma are announced here.
Quuquuatsa Language Group - Many interesting resources and programs found here, follow their facebook page for even more content.
